About Yari

Yari was founded in 2014 in the purpose of linking the physical world (paper based marketing channels, ..) to the online world. With our great talents in computer vision and machine learning fields this reality became true. A user starts by snapping a product/advertisement picture using our mobile application, then he will get redirected to the its purchase page or whatever metadata given for this product picture. This is done using the most scalable and accurate content based image recognition technology.
